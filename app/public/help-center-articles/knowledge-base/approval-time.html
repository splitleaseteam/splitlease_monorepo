How Long Does It Take to Approve a Rental Application?

In general, it will take one to three days for a potential landlord or property manager to approve your application.

The rental application approval process typically takes 1-3 business days, though this can vary based on several factors.

Typical Timeline

  • 24-48 hours: Average for most rentals
  • Same day: Possible with efficient landlords or property managers
  • Up to 7 days: If additional verification is needed

What Affects Processing Time?

1. Background Check Completion

Credit and criminal background checks can take 24-72 hours depending on the service used.

2. Reference Verification

Calling previous landlords and employers takes time, especially if they're slow to respond.

3. Number of Applicants

If multiple people are applying, the landlord reviews them in order received or all at once.

4. Landlord's Schedule

Individual landlords may take longer than professional property management companies.

5. Application Completeness

Missing documents or information delays the process.

How to Speed Up Approval

  • Submit a complete application with all required documents
  • Provide accurate contact information for references
  • Alert previous landlords that they may be contacted
  • Be responsive to any follow-up questions
  • Apply during business hours for faster processing

What Happens During the Wait?

While you wait, the landlord is:

  • Running your credit report
  • Conducting criminal background checks
  • Verifying your employment and income
  • Contacting previous landlords
  • Comparing you to other applicants

When to Follow Up

If you haven't heard back after 3 business days, it's appropriate to send a polite follow-up email or call.
