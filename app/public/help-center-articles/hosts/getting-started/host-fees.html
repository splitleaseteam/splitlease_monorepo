What fees are there for hosts?

Split Lease charges a simple service fee on bookings. Here's everything you need to know about host fees, payouts, and what you actually take home.

Standard Host Fee

10-15% of the booking subtotal. You keep 85-90% of every dollar earned.

The Service Fee Explained

What It Covers

  • Payment processing: Credit card, ACH, international transactions
  • Platform technology: Booking system, calendar, messaging
  • Customer support: 24/7 support for you and guests
  • Marketing: Attracting quality guests to the platform
  • Insurance & trust: Payment protection and dispute resolution
  • Legal compliance: Contracts, agreements, regulatory requirements

How It's Calculated

Simple formula:

  • Guest pays total amount
  • Split Lease deducts 10-15% service fee
  • Remaining 85-90% goes to you
  • Automatic, transparent calculation

Fee Examples

Example 1: Monthly Booking

  • Guest booking: 12 nights @ $100/night = $1,200
  • Service fee (12%): $144
  • Your payout: $1,056

Example 2: Higher Volume

  • Guest booking: 16 nights @ $95/night = $1,520
  • Service fee (10%): $152
  • Your payout: $1,368

Note: Higher volume hosts often get lower fee rates

When Fees Are Deducted

Booking Timeline

  1. Guest books: Pays full amount to Split Lease
  2. Booking confirmed: Money held in escrow
  3. Guest arrives: Funds released to you (minus service fee)
  4. Monthly payout: Transferred to your bank account

Payout Schedule

  • Standard: Monthly automatic payouts
  • Timing: Within 3-5 business days of month end
  • Method: Direct deposit to bank account
  • Currency: USD (international conversions available)

Maximizing Your Earnings

Volume Discounts

Host more, pay less:

  • New hosts: 15% fee
  • $10k+ annual: 12% fee
  • $25k+ annual: 10% fee
  • Multiple properties: Volume pricing applies across portfolio

Long-Term Guests

Lower admin costs = lower fees:

  • 6+ month bookings may qualify for reduced fee
  • Less turnover = more profit
  • Stable income with predictable fees

Special Situations

Damage Charges

If guest causes damage:

  • Charge guest for repairs
  • Split Lease facilitates payment
  • No service fee on damage charges
  • 100% goes to you

Cleaning Fees

If you charge cleaning separately:

  • Cleaning fees are subject to service fee
  • Alternative: include cleaning in nightly rate
  • Or offer self-cleaning option

Additional Services

Extra charges (parking, pets, etc.):

  • Subject to service fee if processed through platform
  • Keeps everything documented and protected
