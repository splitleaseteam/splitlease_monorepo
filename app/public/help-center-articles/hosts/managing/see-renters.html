How can I see who wants to rent my place?

Split Lease makes it easy to view potential renters, screen them, and decide who you want to host. You have complete control over who stays in your property.

You Choose Your Guests

Review every inquiry and booking request. Accept or decline based on your criteria. You're always in control.

How Booking Requests Work

Two Types of Inquiries

1. Direct Booking Request

  • Guest found your listing and wants to book
  • Includes dates, schedule, message
  • You have 48 hours to respond
  • Accept or decline with message

2. General Inquiry

  • Guest has questions before booking
  • No commitment yet
  • Respond to build relationship
  • May lead to booking request

Where to See Requests

Dashboard Overview

  • Log into Split Lease account
  • Go to Dashboard
  • See all pending requests
  • New inquiries highlighted
  • Number badge shows unread count

Email Notifications

  • Instant email when new request arrives
  • Includes guest's basic info
  • Link to view full request
  • Set notification preferences in settings

Mobile App

  • Push notifications for new requests
  • Review and respond on the go
  • Same features as desktop

What You See About Guests

Profile Information

  • Name: First and last name
  • Photo: Profile picture (if provided)
  • Member since: How long on platform
  • Bio: About themselves
  • Verified ID: Government ID verified
  • Verified payment: Payment method confirmed

Rental History

  • Past bookings: Number of stays
  • Reviews: From previous hosts
  • Rating: Overall guest score
  • Hosting experience: Are they also a host?

Booking Details

  • Requested dates/schedule
  • Duration of stay
  • Purpose (work commute, school, etc.)
  • Personal message to you
  • Number of guests (if applicable)

How to Screen Guests

Review Their Profile

  • Read bio carefully
  • Look at profile photo
  • Check member duration
  • Verify all verifications completed

Read Reviews

Look for:

  • Cleanliness comments
  • Communication quality
  • Rule compliance
  • Would hosts host again?
  • Any red flags or concerns

Review Booking History

  • New guests (0 reviews): Higher risk, but everyone starts somewhere
  • 1-3 bookings: Some track record
  • 5+ bookings: Established guest
  • 10+ bookings: Very experienced, usually great

Read Their Message

Good signs:

  • Detailed message about themselves
  • Explains purpose of stay
  • Asks thoughtful questions
  • Professional and friendly tone
  • Shows they read your listing

Red flags:

  • Very brief or generic message
  • Didn't read listing (asks about things already stated)
  • Pushy or demanding tone
  • Asks to go off-platform
  • Requests that violate your rules

Ask Questions

Good screening questions:

  • "What brings you to [city]?"
  • "Tell me about your work schedule"
  • "Have you done Split Lease before?"
  • "What are you looking for in a living situation?"
  • "Any questions about the house rules?"

Accepting Requests

How to Accept

  1. Review guest profile thoroughly
  2. Click "Accept Request"
  3. Send welcome message
  4. Guest is notified
  5. Guest has 24 hours to confirm and pay
  6. Booking finalizes once paid

What Happens Next

  • Lease agreement generated
  • Both parties sign digitally
  • Payment processed
  • Guest receives property details
  • Booking appears on your calendar

Declining Requests

When to Decline

  • Guest doesn't meet your standards
  • Bad reviews or red flags
  • Unverified account
  • Requested dates no longer work
  • Gut feeling something's off

How to Decline Politely

Template messages:

  • "Thank you for your interest. Unfortunately, those dates are no longer available."
  • "Thanks for reaching out. I don't think my space would be the right fit for your needs."
  • "I appreciate your inquiry, but I've accepted another booking for that period."

Don't Over-Explain

  • Simple, polite decline is sufficient
  • Don't give specific reasons (avoid discrimination issues)
  • Don't engage in arguments
  • Just decline and move on

Response Time Matters

48-Hour Window

  • You have 48 hours to respond to requests
  • Faster response = better experience
  • Aim for within 24 hours
  • If request expires, guest can request again

Response Rate

  • Your response rate is public
  • Shows on your listing profile
  • Higher rate = more trust
  • Aim for 90%+ response rate

Acceptance Rate

  • Percentage of requests you accept
  • Not publicly visible
  • Very low rate may trigger review
  • It's okay to be selective, but declining everyone may indicate listing issues

Pre-Approval Feature

When to Use

If guest inquired but didn't book yet:

  • You really like them
  • Want to encourage booking
  • Show you're interested

How It Works

  1. Click "Pre-Approve" on inquiry
  2. Guest receives notification
  3. They have 24 hours to book at listed price
  4. Booking guaranteed if they proceed

Special Requests

Custom Pricing

  • Guest asks for discount
  • You can offer special rate
  • Send custom quote
  • Only for that specific booking

Modified Schedule

  • Guest wants different days than listed
  • You can negotiate schedule
  • Update booking details
  • Guest approves changes

Red Flags to Watch

Profile Red Flags

  • No profile photo
  • Brand new account (created today)
  • No verifications
  • No reviews and vague profile

Communication Red Flags

  • Asks to communicate off-platform
  • Wants to pay outside Split Lease
  • Pushy about booking immediately
  • Won't answer basic questions
  • Story doesn't make sense

Review Red Flags

  • Multiple bad reviews
  • Comments about cleanliness issues
  • Rules violations mentioned
  • Communication problems
  • Hosts wouldn't host again

Best Practices

Be Responsive

  • Check dashboard daily
  • Enable notifications
  • Respond within 24 hours
  • Even if declining, respond promptly

Be Friendly

  • Warm welcome message
  • Answer questions thoroughly
  • Be professional but personable
  • Remember, guest is vetting you too!

Trust Your Gut

  • If something feels off, decline
  • Don't feel pressure to accept
  • Your home, your choice
  • Better to wait for right guest

Give New Guests a Chance

  • Everyone starts with 0 reviews
  • If profile is complete and they seem genuine, consider it
  • Maybe start with trial night
  • Help build the community

Managing Multiple Requests

Same Dates

  • First come, first served (generally)
  • Or choose best fit
  • Accept one, politely decline others
  • Respond to all, even declines

Prioritization

  • Guests with great reviews
  • Verified accounts
  • Detailed, thoughtful messages
  • Long-term commitment

Summary

Screening Guests

  • View all requests in Dashboard
  • See guest profiles, reviews, verification status
  • 48 hours to respond to requests
  • Screen carefully: reviews, history, message quality
  • Accept great fits, politely decline others
  • Trust your gut - your home, your choice
  • Response time matters for your reputation

Take your time to screen guests properly. Finding the right fit makes hosting enjoyable for everyone.
