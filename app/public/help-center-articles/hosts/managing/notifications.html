How do I get notified about bookings and messages?

Split Lease sends notifications through multiple channels so you never miss important updates about your listings, bookings, and guests.

Stay Connected

Get instant alerts for booking requests, messages, payments, and more through email, SMS, and push notifications.

Types of Notifications

Booking-Related

  • New booking request: Guest wants to book your place
  • Booking confirmed: Guest completed payment
  • Booking modification: Request to change dates/schedule
  • Booking cancelled: Guest cancelled booking
  • Upcoming check-in: Guest arrives tomorrow
  • Upcoming check-out: Guest leaves tomorrow

Message-Related

  • New message: Guest sent you a message
  • Inquiry: Potential guest has questions
  • Unanswered message: Reminder if you haven't responded

Payment-Related

  • Payment received: Guest paid rent
  • Payout scheduled: Money being sent to you
  • Payout completed: Money deposited to your account
  • Payment issue: Problem with guest's payment

Review-Related

  • New review: Guest left you a review
  • Review reminder: You can now review guest
  • Review deadline: Review period ending soon

Account-Related

  • Profile updates: Changes to your listing
  • Calendar updates: Availability changed
  • Verification: ID or payment verification status
  • Policy updates: Changes to Split Lease terms

Issues and Support

  • Guest complaint: Guest reported an issue
  • Property issue: Guest reported maintenance problem
  • Support response: Split Lease team replied to your ticket
  • Dispute filed: Formal dispute opened

Notification Channels

Email Notifications

How it works:

  • Sent to your registered email
  • Instant delivery for urgent items
  • Daily digest option for less urgent
  • Includes relevant details and action links
  • Archive for record-keeping

Pros:

  • Detailed information
  • Easy to search and reference later
  • Works on any device
  • No app required

SMS Text Messages

How it works:

  • Sent to your phone number
  • For time-sensitive notifications only
  • Brief summary with link to full details
  • Verify phone number to enable

Pros:

  • Immediate attention
  • See without opening app
  • Works without internet
  • Great for urgent matters

Push Notifications

How it works:

  • Through Split Lease mobile app
  • Pop up on your phone/tablet
  • Tap to go directly to relevant page
  • Customizable per notification type

Pros:

  • Instant and visible
  • Direct link to app
  • Badge count shows unread
  • Free (no SMS charges)

In-App Notifications

How it works:

  • Visible when logged into Split Lease
  • Bell icon shows unread count
  • Full notification history
  • Mark as read/unread

Pros:

  • Complete notification record
  • Never miss anything
  • Review past notifications
  • Organized by type and date

Customizing Notifications

Access Settings

  1. Log into Split Lease account
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications
  3. See all notification types
  4. Toggle each channel on/off
  5. Save preferences

Recommended Settings

High Priority (Enable All):

  • New booking requests (Email, SMS, Push)
  • New messages (Email, Push)
  • Payment issues (Email, SMS, Push)
  • Guest check-in reminders (Email, Push)

Medium Priority (Email + Push):

  • Booking confirmations
  • Payout notifications
  • New reviews
  • Calendar reminders

Low Priority (Email Only or Digest):

  • Marketing updates
  • Tips and suggestions
  • Platform news
  • Monthly summaries

Quiet Hours

  • Set time period for no notifications
  • Example: 10 PM - 7 AM
  • Urgent items (payment issues) may still come through
  • Others held until quiet hours end

Managing Notification Volume

Too Many Notifications?

Solutions:

  • Daily digest: Combine low-priority into one email
  • Disable marketing: Turn off promotional messages
  • Limit SMS: Only for most critical items
  • Bundle similar: Combine related notifications

Multiple Properties

  • Set preferences per property
  • Different email addresses for different properties
  • Tag notifications by property
  • Filter in inbox

Response Time Expectations

Booking Requests

  • Goal: Respond within 24 hours
  • Maximum: 48 hours before expires
  • Impact: Response rate shown on listing

Messages

  • Goal: Reply within 12 hours
  • Impact: Affects response rate
  • Guest expectation: Same-day response

Payment Issues

  • Urgent: Address within 24 hours
  • Risk: Booking may cancel if unresolved

Maintenance Problems

  • Emergency: Immediate (heat, water, security)
  • Non-emergency: Within 48 hours

Notification Best Practices

Check Regularly

  • Review dashboard daily
  • Check email/app at least twice daily
  • Respond to time-sensitive items quickly
  • Don't let messages pile up

Enable Critical Alerts

  • Always enable for booking requests
  • Always enable for payment issues
  • Always enable for guest messages

Use Mobile App

  • Download Split Lease app
  • Enable push notifications
  • Respond on the go
  • Stay connected while traveling

Keep Contact Info Updated

  • Current email address
  • Verified phone number
  • Update if changes
  • Test notifications periodically

Troubleshooting

Not Receiving Notifications

Check:

  • Email in spam/junk folder
  • Notification settings enabled
  • Email/phone number correct
  • Phone number verified
  • App permissions granted (push)

Too Many Notifications

Solutions:

  • Switch to daily digest
  • Disable non-essential types
  • Turn off marketing emails
  • Use email filters/labels

Missing Critical Alerts

Ensure:

  • SMS enabled for urgent items
  • Push notifications allowed
  • Email whitelist splitlease.com
  • Check notification history in app

Email Management

Create Filters

Gmail example:

  • Label "Split Lease - Urgent" for booking requests, messages
  • Label "Split Lease - FYI" for confirmations, updates
  • Archive marketing to separate folder

Whitelist Split Lease

  • Add notifications@splitlease.com to contacts
  • Mark as "Not spam" if in junk
  • Create filter to never send to spam

Vacation/Away Mode

Going on Vacation?

  • Set auto-reply message
  • Block calendar if not available
  • Notifications continue (just delayed response)
  • Or delegate to co-host

Co-Host Notifications

  • Add co-host to listing
  • They receive same notifications
  • Backup when you're unavailable
  • Great for managing multiple properties

Summary

Notification Management

  • Multiple channels: Email, SMS, push, in-app
  • Customize per notification type
  • Enable critical alerts (bookings, messages, payments)
  • Use daily digest for low-priority items
  • Set quiet hours for peace
  • Respond within 24 hours for best results
  • Check dashboard daily

Proper notification management keeps you informed without overwhelming you. Find the right balance for your hosting style.
