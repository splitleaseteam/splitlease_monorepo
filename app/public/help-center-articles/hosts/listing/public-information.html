What information about my place is publicly visible?

Split Lease balances transparency for guests with privacy for hosts. Here's exactly what's public vs. private.

Your Privacy Protected

Exact address and personal contact info are never publicly visible until after booking.

What IS Publicly Visible

Location (General Area)

  • Neighborhood: "Williamsburg, Brooklyn"
  • Approximate map pin: Within 2-3 block radius
  • NOT shown: Exact street address
  • Why: Guests need to know general area for commute planning

Photos

  • All photos you upload are public
  • Interior and exterior shots
  • Remove any personal items/identifiers before photographing
  • Don't include photos with address numbers visible

Property Details

  • Type (entire apartment, private room, etc.)
  • Number of bedrooms/bathrooms
  • Square footage (if you provide)
  • Floor/unit number: Your choice whether to share

Amenities

  • WiFi, parking, kitchen, laundry, etc.
  • Building features (elevator, doorman, gym)
  • All amenities you list are public

Availability Calendar

  • Which nights are available
  • Which nights are already booked (blocked out)
  • Your recurring schedule pattern

Pricing

  • Nightly rate
  • Cleaning fees (if any)
  • Any additional charges

House Rules

  • Smoking policy
  • Pet policy
  • Guest policy
  • Any other rules you set

Your Host Profile

  • First name only
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Bio/description
  • Response rate
  • Member since date
  • Number of properties

Reviews

  • All guest reviews are public
  • Your responses to reviews
  • Overall rating
  • Number of bookings

What is PRIVATE Until Booking

Contact Information

  • Phone number: Only shared after booking
  • Email: Only shared after booking
  • Before booking: All communication through platform messaging

Exact Address

  • Full street address: Revealed after booking confirmed
  • Unit/apartment number: Shared upon booking
  • Access instructions: Sent 24 hours before check-in

Access Details

  • Door codes
  • Lockbox locations
  • Key pickup instructions
  • Building entry procedures

Personal Details

  • Last name
  • Full phone number
  • Personal email
  • Other properties you own (unless you choose to share)

What You Can Control

Optional Information

You decide whether to share:

  • Approximate age
  • Occupation
  • Interests/hobbies
  • Languages spoken
  • Why you're hosting

Description Detail Level

  • Be as specific or vague as comfortable
  • Share neighborhood highlights without identifying exact location
  • Describe building type without giving away address

After Booking Confirmed

Guest Receives

  • Full street address
  • Unit/apartment number
  • Your phone number
  • Check-in instructions
  • WiFi password
  • Parking details
  • Any other access info

Timeline

  • Upon booking: Exact address revealed
  • 24 hours before: Access instructions sent
  • Day of check-in: Your phone number shared

Who Can See Your Listing

Before Booking

  • Anyone: Can browse and see public info
  • Registered users: Can message you
  • You control: Who you respond to and accept

Search Visibility

  • Your listing appears in relevant searches
  • Guests can filter by neighborhood, price, amenities
  • Can't be found by exact address (it's not public)

Managing Your Privacy

Profile Settings

  • Choose what optional info to share
  • Control profile photo visibility
  • Set communication preferences

Listing Settings

  • Hide calendar availability if desired
  • Require guests to send request (not instant book)
  • Screen guests before accepting

Safety Features

  • Verified guests: ID and payment verification required
  • Secure messaging: All communication logged
  • Review system: See guest's booking history
  • Support team: Report any concerning behavior

Summary

Public vs. Private

  • Public: General location, photos, pricing, amenities
  • Private: Exact address, contact info, access details
  • Revealed: After booking is confirmed
  • Protected: Platform ensures privacy and safety
