Can I list more than one property/room?

Absolutely! Split Lease encourages multi-property hosting. There's no limit to how many listings you can create.

Unlimited Listings

List as many properties, rooms, or spaces as you want - all from one account.

Benefits of Multiple Listings

  • Scale your income: More properties = more earnings
  • Diversify risk: One vacancy doesn't hurt as much
  • Volume discounts: Lower service fees for high-volume hosts
  • Build hosting business: Turn hosting into real income stream
  • Leverage experience: What you learn on one applies to all

How to Add Multiple Listings

  1. Log into your Split Lease account
  2. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí "Add New Listing"
  3. Create each listing separately with unique photos, description, pricing
  4. Manage all from one central dashboard
  5. Set different availability calendars for each

    Best Practices for Multi-Property Hosts

    1. Standardize Systems

    • Use same cleaning service for all properties
    • Consistent communication templates
    • Uniform check-in/check-out process
    • Easier to scale when systems are consistent

    2. Differentiate Your Listings

    • Clear unique titles ("Sunny Studio in Williamsburg" vs "Spacious Loft in DUMBO")
    • Unique photos for each
    • Highlight what makes each special
    • Don't confuse guests with generic descriptions

    3. Price Strategically

    • Premium property at top of market rate
    • Mid-tier property at competitive rate
    • Budget option priced to fill quickly
    • Create portfolio that captures different guest segments

    4. Track Performance

    • Monitor which properties book best
    • Identify which guests prefer which spaces
    • Reinvest earnings into top performers
    • Consider selling underperformers

    Scaling Your Business

    Start Small, Grow Smart

    Recommended path:

    1. List 1 property: Learn the platform and hosting
    2. Perfect your system: Get great reviews, dial in operations
    3. Add property 2-3: Apply what you learned
    4. Hire help: Cleaning, maintenance, guest communication
    5. Scale to 5-10+: Now it's a real business

    When to Hire Help

    Consider assistance at:

    • 3+ properties: Part-time cleaning coordination
    • 5+ properties: Virtual assistant for guest communication
    • 10+ properties: Full-time property manager

    Common Questions

    Q: Can I list rooms in my own home while living there?
    A: Yes! Many hosts rent spare bedrooms. Learn more

    Q: Do I need separate accounts for each property?
    A: No, manage all from one account.

    Q: Can I have different co-hosts for different properties?
    A: Yes, assign different team members to different listings.

    Q: Is there a fee per listing?
    A: No, unlimited listings at no cost. You only pay service fee on actual bookings.
