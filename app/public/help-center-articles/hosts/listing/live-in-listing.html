Can I list my place if I live in it and just have extra space?

Yes! Many successful Split Lease hosts rent out spare bedrooms while living in the same home. This is a great way to offset rent/mortgage costs.

House Hacking with Split Lease

Rent your spare room part-time. Guest only there certain days, you have the space to yourself other times.

How Live-in Hosting Works

Typical setup:

  • You live in master bedroom
  • Guest rents spare bedroom Mon-Wed nights
  • Shared common areas (kitchen, living room, bathroom)
  • Clear house rules and boundaries
  • Guest treats it respectfully (knows you live there)

Benefits of Live-in Hosting

  • Offset your rent: Guest pays $800/month, reduces your housing cost
  • Security: You're there to monitor the space
  • Easier management: On-site to handle issues
  • Build relationships: Get to know your guest
  • Flexible schedule: Coordinate with guest's schedule

Best Practices

1. Set Clear Expectations

  • Define shared vs. private spaces
  • Kitchen usage rules
  • Bathroom schedule if shared
  • Noise/quiet hours
  • Guest policies (can they have visitors?)

2. Choose Compatible Guests

  • Professional travelers work best (minimal use, respectful)
  • Similar lifestyle/schedule to yours
  • Trial night is essential for live-in situations
  • Screen carefully - you'll be living together

3. Maintain Privacy

  • Install lock on your bedroom door
  • Designate storage areas
  • Set boundaries about common spaces
  • Respect each other's schedules

4. Be a Good Housemate

  • Keep common areas clean
  • Communicate openly
  • Be flexible and understanding
  • Respect their space as much as yours

What to Include in Your Listing

  • Be clear it's shared: "Private bedroom in occupied home"
  • Describe common areas: "Shared kitchen and bathroom"
  • Set expectations: "Quiet professional building"
  • Your schedule: "Host works from home Mon-Fri"
  • Photos: Show guest's private room + shared spaces

Pricing for Live-in Listings

Typically 30-50% less than entire place:

  • Entire apartment in your area: $2,500/month
  • Private room, shared common areas: $1,200-1,500/month
  • Lower price reflects shared nature
  • Still great income for unused space!

Success Stories

"I rent my spare bedroom to a consultant who's only here Tue-Thu nights. She's super respectful, barely uses the kitchen, and pays $900/month. It cut my rent in half!" - Alex, Chicago

Summary

Live-in Hosting Tips

  • Yes, you can list while living there
  • Set clear boundaries and rules
  • Choose compatible guests
  • Price 30-50% below entire place
  • Great way to offset your housing costs
