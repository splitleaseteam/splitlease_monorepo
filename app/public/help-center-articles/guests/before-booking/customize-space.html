Can I customize my space? Bring a monitor? Rearrange furniture?

Yes, most hosts welcome reasonable customizations! Since you're a recurring guest, hosts understand you need the space to feel like home. The key is communication and respect.

Golden Rule

Always ask your host before making changes. Most say yes to reasonable requests, especially when you explain how it will improve your stay.

Common Customizations (Usually Allowed)

‚úÖ Bringing Your Own Items

Monitors & Tech Equipment
Nearly all hosts allow this, especially for work purposes:

  • External monitor or dual monitor setup
  • Laptop stand and ergonomic keyboard
  • Webcam and ring light for video calls
  • Desk lamp or task lighting

Personal Comfort Items

  • Your own pillow, blankets, or bedding
  • Slippers, bathrobe, towels
  • Coffee maker or tea kettle (if not provided)
  • Personal toiletries and grooming items

Work Essentials

  • Printer/scanner (small, portable)
  • Filing organizer or desk accessories
  • Whiteboard or corkboard
  • Books and reference materials

Pro Tip for Remote Workers

Many hosts specifically market to remote workers and already have monitor-friendly desks with cable management. Look for listings that mention "workspace" or "remote-work ready" in the description.

‚úÖ Minor Furniture Rearrangement

Most hosts allow small adjustments:

  • Moving a desk closer to a window for natural light
  • Repositioning a chair for better workspace ergonomics
  • Adjusting nightstand or lamp placement
  • Rearranging kitchen items for easier access

Best Practice: Take photos of the original layout so you can return everything before you leave.

‚úÖ Temporary Additions

Non-permanent enhancements are usually fine:

  • Command strips for hanging lightweight items
  • Removable hooks for bags, coats, or towels
  • Non-slip rug pads
  • Cord organizers and cable clips

When in Doubt, Ask

Hosts appreciate guests who communicate. A quick text or email prevents misunderstandings and shows you respect their property.

How to Ask Your Host

Before Booking (Ideal Timing)

"Hi! I'm interested in your listing for my Tuesday-Thursday work schedule. I work remotely and would like to bring an external monitor and possibly rearrange the desk slightly for better ergonomics. Would you be open to this?"

After Booking (If Plans Change)

"I've realized I'd be more productive if I could bring a small printer. It's compact and I'd keep it on the desk. Would that work for you?"

What Hosts Love About Customization

Many hosts actually encourage personalization because:

  • Happy guests stay longer: When you're comfortable, you're more likely to renew
  • Shows you care: Asking permission demonstrates respect
  • Improves the space: Your suggestions might benefit future guests
  • Builds relationships: Collaborative problem-solving creates trust

Real Example

One guest asked to bring a standing desk converter. The host loved the idea and now includes it in the listing for all guests. Another guest's monitor setup became a selling point in the host's photos!

Items Hosts Usually Say No To

  • Pets (unless listing is pet-friendly)
  • Exercise equipment (heavy, bulky, noise concerns)
  • Major appliances (space heaters, portable AC units, etc.)
  • Smoking or vaping items (most rentals are non-smoking)
  • Large entertainment systems (unless replacing existing setup)

Storage for Your Items

Since you'll be leaving items at the rental:

  • Ask about dedicated storage space (closet shelf, drawer, cabinet)
  • Use clear bins or bags to keep your items organized
  • Label your belongings clearly
  • Don't take up excessive space in shared storage areas

See also: Can I store my things at my Split Lease?

End of Stay: Return to Original

When your arrangement ends:

  • Return furniture to original positions (use your photos!)
  • Remove all Command strips, hooks, and temporary items
  • Take all your personal belongings
  • Leave the space as you found it (or better!)

Build Trust

The better you treat the space and communicate with your host, the more flexible they'll be with customizations. It's a relationship built on mutual respect!
