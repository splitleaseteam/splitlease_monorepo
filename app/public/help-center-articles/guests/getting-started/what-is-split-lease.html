What is Split Lease and what do you do?

Split Lease is a rental marketplace platform (Market Network) that creates a new accommodation category for multi-local living - people who regularly spend time in 2+ cities.

In Simple Terms

We help you rent a place part-time in another city, so you have a consistent "second home" without paying for nights you're not there.

What Makes Split Lease Different?

Unlike traditional travel accommodations or permanent housing, Split Lease enables flexible, part-time rentals with long-term host-guest relationships.

Core Features:

  • Split Schedules - Multiple guests can alternate use of the same property (e.g., one guest Monday-Wednesday, another Thursday-Sunday)
  • Zero Risk for Hosts - No upfront fees to list your property
  • Light-Usage Focus - Guests treat spaces as secondary addresses, not primary homes
  • Flexible Booking - Change dates and negotiate schedules (unlike Airbnb's rigid model)
  • Store Your Belongings - Leave your things at your rental between visits

Who Uses Split Lease?

Guests (Multi-Locals)

People who need consistent accommodations in multiple cities:

  • Corporate consultants who travel to the same city weekly
  • Remote workers with hybrid schedules
  • Startup founders splitting time between cities
  • Weekend commuters working in major metros
  • Anyone living a multi-local lifestyle

Typical Budget

Most guests pay $70-120/night depending on location and density. Since you're only paying for nights you actually use, it's significantly cheaper than maintaining a full-time second apartment.

Hosts (Property Owners)

People who want to monetize their space part-time:

  • NYU students renting Manhattan apartments on weekends
  • Part-time residents living in their space some days of the week
  • Property owners who want "light usage" guests, not high-maintenance Airbnb customers
  • Anyone with extra space they're not using full-time

How Split Lease Works

1. Search for Listings

Browse available properties in your target city. Filter by neighborhood, amenities, and your specific days/schedule.

2. Try a Trial Night

Book a single night to test the space before committing to a longer arrangement. This lets you verify the location, meet the host, and ensure it's the right fit.

3. Book Your Schedule

Reserve your preferred days (e.g., every Monday-Thursday, or every other week). You only pay for the nights you'll actually stay.

4. Store Your Belongings

Leave clothing, toiletries, work equipment, or personal items at your rental. No need to lug suitcases back and forth!

5. Adjust as Needed

Life changes? Request amendments to your booking dates. Split Lease offers flexibility that traditional rentals and Airbnb don't.

What is Periodic Tenancy?

Split Lease operates under periodic tenancy rather than short-term rentals. This means:

  • No surprise fees - No cleaning fees on every booking
  • Better pricing - Save money with 30+ night commitments
  • Legal protections - Proper rental agreements for both parties
  • Long-term relationships - Build trust with your host over time

Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a money-back guarantee to ensure your satisfaction. If the space doesn't meet your expectations, we've got you covered.

Our Mission

Split Lease is building a market network for recurring travel and multi-locality. We're creating an entirely new accommodation category between traditional travel (Airbnb) and permanent housing.

We believe the future of living is multi-local - and we're making it affordable and accessible for everyone.
