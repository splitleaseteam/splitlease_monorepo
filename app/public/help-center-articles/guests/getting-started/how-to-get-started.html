What's the easiest way to get started?

Getting started with Split Lease is simple. Follow these 5 easy steps to find your perfect part-time rental.

Step 1: Identify Your Needs

Before browsing listings, clarify what you're looking for:

  • Which city? Where do you need a second home?
  • Which days? Weekdays? Weekends? Specific days of the week?
  • How often? Every week? Every other week? Monthly?
  • What's your budget? Most guests pay $70-120/night

Pro Tip

The more specific you are about your schedule, the easier it is to find the perfect match.

Step 2: Browse Available Listings

Visit app.split.lease/search to explore rentals:

  • Filter by neighborhood and amenities
  • Select your preferred days of the week
  • View photos and detailed descriptions
  • Check host profiles and reviews

Step 3: Request a Market Report (Optional)

Not sure what's available in your area? Request a free market report to see:

  • Average pricing for your target neighborhood
  • Available inventory matching your schedule
  • Personalized recommendations

Step 4: Book a Trial Night

Found a promising listing? Start with a trial night:

  • Book just one night to test the space
  • Meet the host in person
  • Verify the location works for you
  • Check amenities and neighborhood

Why Trial Nights?

Trial nights let you "test drive" before committing to a longer booking. Most guests love this low-risk way to find the perfect fit.

Step 5: Book Your Schedule

Once you've found the right place:

  1. Select your preferred days and frequency
  2. Review pricing and terms
  3. Submit your booking request
  4. Wait for host approval (usually within 24-48 hours)
  5. Complete payment and move in!

What Happens Next?

After your booking is confirmed:

  • Receive check-in instructions from your host
  • Get access details and house rules
  • Coordinate key pickup or access codes
  • Start enjoying your new second home!

Need Help?

Our team is here to assist you every step of the way:

  • Email: support@split.lease
  • Live Chat: Available on our website
  • Response Time: Usually within 24 hours

First Time Using Split Lease?

We recommend starting with a trial night or a short-term booking (1-2 weeks) before committing to a longer arrangement.
