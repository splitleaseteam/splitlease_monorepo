Do I have to travel for work to use Split Lease?

No, you don't need to travel for work to use Split Lease! While many of our guests are business travelers, Split Lease serves anyone who regularly spends time in multiple locations.

The Bottom Line

Split Lease is for anyone who needs a consistent second home - whether that's for work, family, lifestyle, or personal reasons.

Who Uses Split Lease?

1. Work-Related Travel (Common)

  • Consultants visiting clients regularly
  • Remote workers going to office certain days
  • Sales professionals covering territories
  • Startup founders splitting time between cities

2. Family & Personal Reasons (Very Common)

  • Visiting family: Need a place near aging parents every weekend
  • Co-parenting: Split custody requiring presence in another city
  • Long-distance relationships: Regular visits to your partner's city
  • Caring for loved ones: Weekly visits to help family members

3. Lifestyle & Recreation

  • Weekend escapes: Regular getaways to your favorite city
  • Hobby pursuits: Music lessons, art classes, or sports in another city
  • Cultural experiences: Theatre season tickets, museum memberships
  • Social life: Friend groups or community ties in multiple cities

4. Education & Personal Development

  • Graduate students with research in multiple locations
  • Continuing education or certification programs
  • Artists and creatives maintaining studios elsewhere
  • Volunteers with regular commitments in another city

Real Example

One of our guests uses Split Lease every weekend to visit their elderly mother in Brooklyn. Another guest maintains a place in Manhattan for date nights and cultural events, despite living in New Jersey during the week.

What Matters Most

The key factor isn't why you need a second home - it's that you need one regularly and predictably.

You're a great fit for Split Lease if you:

  • Visit the same city on a recurring schedule
  • Know roughly which days you'll be there (even if flexible)
  • Want consistency and your own space (not hotels)
  • Use the space respectfully as a secondary home

Work travel isn't required - just regular travel of any kind!

Popular Use Cases (Non-Work)

The Weekend Commuter
Lives in NYC Monday-Friday for work, goes home to family upstate on weekends. Split Lease provides their weekday place.

The Family Caregiver
Visits aging parents in Boston every other weekend. Split Lease gives them a comfortable, familiar place to stay.

The Culture Enthusiast
Maintains a subscription to NYC Broadway shows and visits twice a month. Split Lease is their Manhattan base.

The Co-Parent
Shares custody requiring presence in another city alternate weeks. Split Lease provides stability for those weeks.

When Work Travel Isn't Involved

Some considerations if you're not traveling for work:

  • Budget: Make sure the rental fits your personal budget (since there's no employer reimbursement)
  • Schedule clarity: Being clear about your recurring pattern helps hosts plan
  • Communication: Share your use case with hosts - many love supporting family visits or personal pursuits

Host Perspective

Many hosts actually prefer non-work travelers! Family visitors and lifestyle guests often take extra care of the space and build meaningful long-term relationships.
